Designed by SFPV.

We collaborated on every aspect of this frame with Colby Curtola, AKA SFPV. We liked Raggio Lungo so much that we wanted a dual-purpose 5" version that could race and/or mount a GoPro, with ultimate camera protection, quick arm swaps, plenty of room, and perfect CoG.

HD ready for your most epic flights

With strap slots for both a normal parallel battery as well as the perpendicular "toilet tank" style, Raggio Veloce can mount a GoPro session up front for HD footage, and it uses the same unique carbon plate sandwich that makes Raggio Lungo's footage so crisp and jello-free.

Spare parts for crash damage recovery.

Each Raggio Veloce kit comes with 5 arms and an extra set of cam protection braces, in case you manage to break something during an unexpected landing.

Not afraid to accessorize.

A host of accessories, such as booties, GoPro mounts, antenna mounts, and more are all available on Thingiverse.

Additionally, Raggio Veloce is compatible with Raggio Lungo arms and accessories, so you can upgrade to 7" arms, add a GPS to the rear, or that sweet RAGGIO underglow.
