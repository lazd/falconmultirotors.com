Garuda 200

Fast, beautiful, maintainable.

An uncompromising quest for beauty.

We wanted to create the most beautiful racing drone you've ever seen without placing form over function.

Garuda has a skeletal structure inspired by nature, curves inspired by Italian supercars, aerodynamics inspired by jet aircraft, and features inspired by Swiss army knives.

FPV and HD-first.

With a built-in 40° HD camera mount and adjustable 30°, 45°, and 60° FPV camera mount, you'll never have to deal with fragile camera mounts and their tiny screws or 3D-printed camera mounts and their zip ties.

Pop in a RunCam Swift or HS1177, strap up a GoPro, Session, Yi, or other HD camera, and Garuda is ready for FPV.

Light-weight: balanced.

When designing Garuda, we paid special attention to weight balance. Even with an HD camera, Garuda never tilts when placed on the ground. With a 1300mAh battery strapped to the bottom and a GoPro on top, Garuda is balanced along the horizontal axis for clean flips and rolls.

At tilt, Garuda maintains a straight line through to GoPro to the battery, giving you a balanced feel while flying at high speeds.

As a true-X, Garuda gives the best possible stick feel with the motors and forces right where the flight controller expects them to be.

Designed with maintenance in mind.

When something goes wrong in the field, Garuda is ready for repair.

Full access to the flight controller, RX, and VTX means you can perform repairs without even removing a screw.

Mythically durable.

Garuda is named after an immortal, ass-kicking bird, and aptly so.

We partnered with ElementalRC to cut Garuda from their hilariously durable radial carbon fiber, which means each layer of is rotated, producing a final product that has no weak points due to the unidirectional nature of carbon fiber.

With a canopy made out of indestructible TPU, Garuda lives up to the legend.

Not afraid to customize and accessorize.

With TPU booties, battery protectors, VTX and RX holders, and optimized canopy designs in any color, Garuda is yours to customize.

Match your props and your canopy, your arm-tip LEDs with your VTX antenna, or your battery with your canopy LEDs, Garuda gives you more options to customize your drone and ditch the "completely black except the props" look most drones have.

Nails the little things.

With zip tie slots and a mounting hole for your XT-60, nut holding nubs for canopy screws, extra-snug hardware-free FPV camera mounting, and included vibration-damping o-rings to mount your flight controller, Garuda misses no details.

We've agonized over every aspect of the frame, especially the little things, with dozens of revisions and changes so minor they're best measured in micrometers.

Compatible with whatever parts you choose.

We've taken into account all flavors of common configurations, providing you hardware and mounting solutions no matter what parts you prefer. The only hardware requirements are 22XX motors and a standard cased FPV camera (HS1177 or RunCam Swift).

Garuda's included hardware lets you mount your PDB and flight controller with plenty of clearance for components and vibration damping with the included o-rings for those sensitive gyros.
