No compromise design.

The idea started simple. We wanted to design an X-quad thatâ€™s lighter, shorter, easier to build, and more durable than anything on the market.

But we wanted a built-in HD camera mount, a PDB that works with Littlebees, support for different cameras and VTXs, easily available spare parts, durability and motor protection, an easily servicable build, and, uh, battery strap slots.

We wanted all of this in the lightest possible package, and it had to look good.

Designed with maintenance in mind.

When something goes wrong in the field, Falcon doesn't require a teardown to fix it. Removing 4 screws gives access to your flight controller, VTX, and camera.

We chose the hardware for Falcon very carefully. Where other frames use a locknut as a spacer, Falcon uses a 4mm threadless aluminum spacer that makes it easy to quickly take the build apart and access the top of the PDB and the bottom of the flight controller.

Changing out a broken battery strap is as simple as backing off the 18mm screws from the main stack (without removing them), then sliding the strap through Falcon's battery strap slots.

3 ways to build it.

The Falcon 160 and 185 can be built 3 different ways, each designed to support a different camera and features.

  • 17mm camera build - designed around the HS1177M, this build has a fixed camera angle that matches the HD camera angle.
  • 25mm camera build - designed around the HS1177, this build has a variable camera angle and an optional HD camera mount.
  • Micro build - designed tiny CMOS cameras, this build has a fixed camera angle and an no option for mounting a HD camera.

Cut from the best carbon fiber in the industry.

We didn't skimp here. Falcon is cut by Armattan's team of skilled CNC operators from the same high quality Armattan carbon fiber you've come to expect. Every cut is clean and smooth. Tolerances are spot on. And, of course, the durability is legendary.

A PDB with a purpose.

Falcon needed a PDB that was designed specifically for X quads. We needed a no compromise, light weight, high amperage PDB with 5V pads in the right location for popular FCs and ESC pads that don't require you to criss-cross wires or flip your ESC upside down.

Nothing like that existed, so we worked with Flynoceros to design the reversible Cloverleaf Pro PDB -- one side is perfect for KISS and Rotorgeeks ESCs, and the other perfect for everything else. With 4oz copper, support for dual Pololu voltage regulators, zip tie slots for your XT60 pigtail, and pads in all the right places, there's nothing else like it, and it ships wiht Falcon 160 and 185.
